Partners in Success: Our Commitment to Your Brand's Growth

At LooseThreads LLC, we don’t just create embroidered apparel—we build lasting partnerships. Your brand’s image is a long-term investment, and we’re committed to making it easy and rewarding to work with us again and again.

Effortless Re-ordering & Project Management

  • No Re-digitization Fees: Your logo digitizing fee is one-time for that logo—future orders use the file on record.
  • Simple Re-ordering: Tell us quantity & product type—we already have your specs and colors.
  • Account History: We securely store your past orders for quick references and planning.

VIP Loyalty Program: Rewarding Our Partners

  • Tiered Discounts: Save more as you grow (bulk & repeat orders).
  • Early Access: Try new products and techniques first.
  • Exclusive Promotions: Members-only deals throughout the year.

Personalized & Proactive Service

  • Check-ins: Periodic follow-ups on how your apparel is performing.
  • Creative Collaboration: Design ideas, thread color matching, and material guidance.
  • Feedback Loops: We listen and continuously improve based on your input.

The Referral Advantage

  • Referral Rewards: Refer a new business—earn a credit toward your next order after their first is complete.
