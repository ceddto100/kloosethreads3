Standard Uniform Embroidery Placements

Strategic placement ensures maximum brand visibility while maintaining professional appearance:

Left Chest Logo

The standard for corporate uniforms. Company logo positioned over the heart. Professional, understated, and universally recognized. Typically 3.5-4" wide.

Right Chest Name

Individual employee name placement. First name only, or full name—your choice. Creates personal accountability and customer connection.

Sleeve Logo

Additional branding opportunity on left or right sleeve. Great for secondary logos, certifications, or American flag patches for government contractors.

Back Yoke

Large logo or company name across the upper back. Maximum visibility for field workers, delivery drivers, and anyone who turns their back to customers.

Collar/Cuff Details

Subtle branding for executive uniforms. Small logo or monogram on collar or cuff for refined professional appearance.

Custom Placement

Non-standard placement for specific applications. We'll advise on what works best for your garment type and intended visibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you handle different names and titles on each uniform?

Absolutely! Send us your roster spreadsheet with names, titles, departments, and sizes. We automate the personalization process, ensuring each garment is correctly embroidered and labeled for the right employee.

What file format do you need for our logo?

Vector files (AI, EPS, PDF, SVG) are ideal. We can also work with high-resolution raster images. If you only have a low-res file, our team can often recreate it for an additional fee.

Do you stock specific uniform brands?

We stock popular brands from Port Authority, Carhartt, Dickies, Cherokee, and more. We can also source specific styles from virtually any supplier, or you can provide your own garments.

How do bulk discounts work?

Volume discounts apply to embroidery fees: 10% off at 12+ pieces, 15% off at 24+ pieces, 20% off at 50+ pieces. Orders of 100+ receive custom enterprise pricing. Discounts apply to the embroidery portion only.

What's the turnaround time for uniform orders?

Standard uniform orders ship in 7-10 business days after proof approval. Larger orders (50+ pieces) may require additional time. Rush service available—contact us to discuss your deadline.

How do I reorder for new hires?

Simply email us with the employee name, size, and quantity needed. Your logo and specifications are already on file—we can typically ship within 3-5 business days for small reorders.

Can you embroider uniforms we already own?

In most cases, yes. Bring in your existing uniforms and we'll assess them. Most commercial garments work well for embroidery. We'll be upfront about any items that may present challenges.

Do you offer samples before large orders?

Yes! For new uniform programs, we strongly recommend sample pieces. You'll see exactly how the embroidery looks on your chosen garments. Sample fees are typically credited toward your full order.

How do I manage sizes for my team?

Provide sizes in your roster spreadsheet. Not sure about sizing? We can provide size charts, or send sample garments for your team to try on before ordering. We'll help you navigate the sizing process.

Is there a minimum order for uniform programs?

No minimum! We serve businesses from 5 employees to 500+. Smaller orders have the same quality standards as large orders. Volume discounts begin at 12 pieces.
